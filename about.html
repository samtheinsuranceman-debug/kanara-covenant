Our Pact

We, the brothers of Kanara, enter into a living covenant — not a contract that can be broken when convenient, but a sacred rhythm that continues even when we falter. This pact unites our faith, our families, our finances, and our future.

The Living Pulse at 90 beats per minute is the audible and visible sign of this agreement. It beats on every page of this site and, we pray, in the hidden chambers of our decisions.

Three Strands

  • Faith — We walk with God in daily surrender and corporate worship.
  • Brotherhood — We calibrate one another in truth, mercy, and accountability (see the Calibration tool).
  • Stewardship — We manage resources with the wisdom of Russell Capital principles: tax-efficient, purpose-driven, legacy-minded wealth that serves the Kingdom.

Why 90 BPM?

Because a covenant that only races or only sleeps is no covenant at all. 90 beats is the tempo of a man who is present — calm enough to hear, strong enough to act, steady enough to endure for decades.

When you feel the pulse on this site, remember: the same heart is beating for you in your work, your marriage, your giving, and your private prayers.
